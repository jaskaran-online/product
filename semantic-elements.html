About Semantic Elements

Semantic elements clearly describe their meaning in a human- and machine-readable way.

What are Semantic Elements?

Published on January 15, 2024

Semantic elements are HTML elements that have meaning beyond just presentation. They help screen readers, search engines, and other tools understand the structure and content of your webpage.

<article>

Benefits of Semantic HTML

Published on January 20, 2024

  • Accessibility: Screen readers can navigate content better
  • SEO: Search engines understand content structure
  • Maintainability: Code is more readable and maintainable
  • Future-proof: Semantic meaning remains constant
